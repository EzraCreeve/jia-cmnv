Our Story

A Church Built
on Family.

Jesus Is Alive CMNV Monumento is an interdenominational, Christ-centered, Bible-believing and Spirit-filled church in Caloocan City. We exist to make disciples, build families, and fill the heavens with worship.

Led by Pastor Stephen Prado, we are part of the broader Jesus Is Alive Community — a movement of churches committed to passionate worship, family spirit, and relentless love for the lost.
